About AtBC

   Above the Board Company has been operational since 2005. We provide Menu Boards, Notice Boards, Counter Mats, Bar Runners, Stubby Holders and other materials for use in; some of the most unique, renowned and high traffic venues like Mine-workers Clubs, R.S.L. Venues, Sporting and Golf Clubs, Defence establishments and defence retirement villages.

    As AtBC we also supply all contracted venues with printed products, upon request. Once a business advertises with us in any of the venues, the participating business immediatley becomes tagged as the preferred supplier within that base area. We create a win-win situation for businesses as well as the venues by limiting the advertisements to only one business per category. Moreover we also offer several advertising vehicles for Sole and Exclusive representation.
